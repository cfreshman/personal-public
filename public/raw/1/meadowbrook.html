GAGA BALL
IT'S KINDA LIKE DODGEBALL hit the ball with your hands to get other players out you win if:  •  you're the final player  •  you have to leave before the end
TO START  •  stand in pit with hand on wall  •  one player tosses ball up into middle  •  yell GA GA BALL as ball bounces 3 times, then play!
YOU'RE OUT IF YOU  •  leave wall or hit ball before 3 bounces  •  ball touches you at knee or below  •  hit ball:     1) twice in a row (double touch) - reset by wall or other player     2) with fist - open hands only     3) into waist or above besides arms/hands     4) into air and someone catches     5) out of pit - last player to touch - unless it was (3)  •  hold/throw ball - drop after catching  •  sit, kneel, or crouch (turtling)
REMEMBER  •  leave pit immediately when out  •  solve issues with rock-paper-scissors  •  winner starts next game  •  be kind and have good sportsmanship!
VARIATIONS revenge: if the player who got you out gets out, you're back in multiball: more than one ball!
